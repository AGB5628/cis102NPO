Our Impact

Since our founding in 1987, we have worked in over 70 countries, supporting farmers, forest communities, and governments to protect biodiversity and reduce climate change impacts.

Our mission has always been rooted in fostering sustainable livelihoods while conserving the planet's precious natural resources. By collaborating with local partners, we strive to implement innovative solutions that balance economic growth with environmental stewardship.

Over the years, our initiatives have included promoting sustainable agricultural practices, advancing reforestation efforts, and empowering communities to adopt climate-resilient strategies.

Improved practices on 6.8 million hectares of farmland

We’ve helped farmers improve their land management practices, increasing crop yields and reducing environmental impact.

Restored critical ecosystems to combat deforestation

Our projects have focused on ecosystem restoration, fighting deforestation and enhancing biodiversity.

Enabled over 2 million farmers to adopt sustainable techniques

Through training and support, we’ve empowered farmers to use sustainable farming techniques that are better for the environment and their livelihoods.
